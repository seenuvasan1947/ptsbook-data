Episode 1: The Fundamental Concept of Adlerian Psychology

Adlerian psychology, developed by Alfred Adler, centers around the idea that human behavior is strongly influenced by our perceptions of ourselves and our place in society. It posits that individuals are driven by the pursuit of significance, which Adler termed  striving for superiority.  This episode delves into the core principles of Adlerian psychology, emphasizing the importance of understanding one's unique style of life and the power to change it. Adler's ideas challenge the notion that our past dictates our future, offering hope for personal transformation.
