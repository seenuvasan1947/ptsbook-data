Episode 10: Psychological Freedom and Responsibility

In this episode, we delve into the idea of psychological freedom and responsibility. Adlerian psychology suggests that individuals have the freedom to choose their responses to life's challenges and events. It encourages readers to take responsibility for their choices and actions, recognizing that they have the power to shape their own destinies. This concept is central to the book's message of empowerment and personal transformation, highlighting the idea that one's past or external circumstances do not define their future.
