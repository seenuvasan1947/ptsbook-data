Episode 9: Inferiority and Superiority Complexes

This episode delves into the concepts of inferiority and superiority complexes as discussed in Adlerian psychology. It explores how these complexes can influence one's behavior and self-perception. Inferiority complex refers to a feeling of inadequacy or lower self-worth, while superiority complex involves an exaggerated sense of superiority or entitlement. The book discusses how these complexes can be obstacles to personal growth and happiness, emphasizing the importance of recognizing and overcoming them.
