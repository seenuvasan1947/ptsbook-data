Episode 12: The Influence of the Past and Changing One's Trajectory

In this  episode, we explore the idea of the influence of the past and the potential for changing one's trajectory in life. The book acknowledges that past experiences and traumas can shape one's beliefs and behaviors. However, it also underscores the Adlerian perspective that individuals have the power to reinterpret their past and make choices that can lead to a different future. It encourages readers to take ownership of their lives, break free from the constraints of the past, and create a more fulfilling and happy future.
