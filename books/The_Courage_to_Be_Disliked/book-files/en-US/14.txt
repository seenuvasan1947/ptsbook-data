Episode 13: The Art of Contribution and Community

In this episode, we delve into the concepts of the art of contribution and the importance of community in one's life. The book emphasizes that individuals have the capacity to make valuable contributions to society and find purpose through helping others. Adlerian psychology underscores the idea that a sense of belonging and community is essential for well-being. This episode explores how fostering a spirit of contribution and connecting with a supportive community can lead to a more fulfilling and content life.
