Episode 8: Relationships and Social Connection

In this episode, we explore the significance of relationships and social connections in one's life. Adlerian psychology underscores the importance of social interest and genuine human connections. The book discusses how fostering meaningful relationships can lead to personal growth and happiness. It challenges the notion that happiness is solely an individual pursuit and encourages readers to prioritize and nurture their relationships, recognizing that social connections play a pivotal role in personal well-being.
