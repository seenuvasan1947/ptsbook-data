Episode 11: Resolving Conflicts and Communication

In this episode, we delve into the important themes of resolving conflicts and effective communication. The book discusses how conflicts are a natural part of human relationships and provides insights into how to address and resolve them. It emphasizes the significance of open and honest communication as a key tool for maintaining healthy relationships. Effective communication, as outlined in Adlerian psychology, involves active listening, empathy, and a willingness to understand the perspectives of others. This episode explores these concepts and their role in fostering harmonious relationships.
