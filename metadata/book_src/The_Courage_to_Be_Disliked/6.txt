Episode 5: The Courage to Be Happy

This episode delves into the profound concept of  The Courage to Be Happy.  It discusses how happiness is not something that should be pursued externally or dependent on others' approval. Instead, the book emphasizes that happiness is a choice, an internal state of mind that individuals can cultivate independent of external circumstances. It challenges the common belief that one's past or current circumstances dictate their happiness, encouraging readers to take responsibility for their own emotional well-being and find the courage to be happy.
